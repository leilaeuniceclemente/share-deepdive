Headline: Business is growing volume strongly (+12% vs prior year), driven by new store expansion and a shift toward premium spend. However, total portfolio share is declining −0.8 pts as competitor and emerging brands are capturing elevated benefit spaces (hydration, repair, premiumization) faster than the current portfolio addresses them. Cat A and Cat B account for 85% of the share gap — these are the priority categories to win back.
Watch: Premium-format stores (roughly 12% of total doors but 22% of sales weight) are the steepest bleeder segment. Emerging brands are structurally over-indexed here; our distribution depth in this tier falls below 50% for the fastest-growing product forms.
